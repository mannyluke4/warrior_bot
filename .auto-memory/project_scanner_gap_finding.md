# Key Finding: Scanner Blindness Is the #1 Gap

The bot's biggest gap isn't bad trade logic — it's scanner blindness. On Jan 2, 2025, the entire $12K gap was explained by one stock (XPON) the bot never saw. The bot's pre-market gap scan misses mid-morning breaking news movers that produce Ross's biggest winners. Scanner coverage is the #1 priority — without seeing the right stocks, nothing else matters.
