## Critical Analysis

### Why the Bot Was Completely Shut Out

**Root Cause 1: MACD Gate**
The ONLY setup the detector found was at 07:02. MACD score was -0.5 (barely bearish). The gate requires positive MACD. This is because:
- Premarket seeded EMA9 at $5.78
- Stock had been at $8.88 premarket high before opening at $5.83
- The gap DOWN from premarket high created a bearish MACD signal
- The bot treated the open as "bearish" when in reality it was the start of a massive run

**Root Cause 2: No More Setups Generated**
After the 07:02 skip, the bot never found another setup. This likely means:
- The move from 07:03 onward was so fast (5 green bars → pullback → 5 more green bars) that the detector's impulse→pullback→trigger state machine never completed a cycle
- Every pullback was immediately followed by another explosive bar before the detector could arm
- The stock was running too fast for the micro pullback pattern to form

**Root Cause 3: Halt Bars Made Post-8AM Untradeable**
The 08:00-08:14 period shows bars with 60-173% ranges — these are LULD circuit breaker halt/resume artifacts. No real setup could form during halts.

### What This Reveals About the Bot's Limitations

1. **MACD gate on fresh runners**: A stock that gaps down from premarket high but opens strong has a "stale bearish" MACD from the seed data. The bot interprets this as "don't buy" when in reality the stock is surging. The MACD is lagging the actual momentum.

2. **Too-fast-to-catch**: When a stock goes from $5.83 to $11.31 in 29 minutes with only brief 1-2 bar pullbacks, the micro pullback detector can't build a setup. The detector needs: impulse → pullback (minimum bars) → trigger. If the "pullback" is only 1 bar before another explosion, the state machine resets.

3. **No halt awareness**: The bot doesn't know when a stock is halted vs actively trading. Halt bars produce garbage data (173% ranges in a single minute).

4. **Premarket context missing**: The bot seeded from 4AM and saw the premarket run to $8.88, then the opening "gap down" to $5.83. But this wasn't a real gap down — it was just premarket profit-taking before the real move. The bot had no way to distinguish this.

### Pattern Profile: "Premarket Runner — Missed by Design"

This is a fundamentally different pattern from ROLR and MLEC:
- **ROLR/MLEC**: Quiet premarket → intraday trigger → multi-leg breakout
- **TWG**: Big premarket run → opens below PM high → resumes running → circuit breaker halts → fade

The bot's detector is designed for the ROLR/MLEC pattern. TWG required:
- Entering during the 07:00-07:08 run before the bot could build a proper setup
- Or entering the recovery at 07:16-07:22 after the first pullback
- Or entering the parabolic extension at 07:23-07:25

All of these would have required either:
- Relaxing the MACD gate for stocks with strong premarket momentum
- A faster setup detection that can arm within 2-3 bars instead of requiring a full impulse→pullback cycle
- A "premarket runner continuation" pattern that recognizes when a PM runner resumes during session

## Key Takeaways for Trade Profiles

### The Premarket Runner Problem

This is now a confirmed gap pattern. TWG is NOT an edge case — premarket runners that continue into the session are a major category of profitable trades. The bot is **structurally unable to trade them** because:

1. **MACD gate**: Premarket run → pullback at open → stale bearish MACD. The gate blocks the first (and often only) setup.
2. **State machine speed**: After the MACD skip, the stock moves too fast for the impulse→pullback→trigger cycle to complete.
3. **No premarket pattern recognition**: Ross identified an inverted H&S forming during the pullback. The bot's pattern detector only runs on session bars.

### What Ross Did That the Bot Can't

| Capability | Ross | Bot | Fix Difficulty |
|---|---|---|---|
| **VWAP break entry** | Used VWAP reclaim as primary trigger | VWAP is tracked but not used as an entry trigger | Medium — VWAP reclaim detection exists conceptually |
| **Inverted H&S recognition** | Saw the pattern forming live | No inverted H&S pattern in detector | Medium — add to patterns.py |
| **Small-to-big scaling** | Started $1K to "break the ice," scaled to 10K shares | Fixed position sizing every time | Part of the larger scaling gap |

### Profile Draft: "Premarket Runner Continuation"

**Identifying Characteristics**:
- Big premarket move (>50% from PM low to PM high)
- Significant premarket volume (>1M shares)
- Pullback from PM high before session open (30-50% retracement)
- Stock "holds up" at open — doesn't make new lows
- VWAP acts as key support/resistance during session

**What the Bot Should Do (Future)**:
- Override MACD gate if PM volume > X and PM range > Y%
- Use VWAP reclaim as primary entry trigger (not micro pullback pattern)
- Start with reduced position size (Ross's "break the ice" approach)
- Scale up on confirmation (break of prior high, break of whole dollars)
- Trail with wider stops — PM runners are volatile but trend strongly

**Current Bot Behavior**:
- MACD gate blocks entry → stock runs away → bot sits idle for 5 hours
- This is the worst possible outcome: a proven runner with $20K+ potential and the bot generates zero trades
