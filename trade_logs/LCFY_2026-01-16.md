## Pattern Profile: "Post-Halt Crash Recovery"

**Identifying characteristics**:
- Micro float with halt on open (stock jumps 50-100%+ in first candle)
- Immediate crash: gives back 50-70% of the halt spike within 15 minutes
- Hits a hard LOD (often near/below the pre-halt open)
- Then a real recovery begins: 10+ consecutive green bars building from the LOD
- Recovery hits resistance at 50-60% of the halt spike range
- Breakout above resistance is the real trade — often runs to/above the halt high

**How LCFY compares to the others**:
