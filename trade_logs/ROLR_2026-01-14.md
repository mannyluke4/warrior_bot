### The Scaling Gap — Biggest Opportunity

Ross's $85K vs the bot's -$889 on the same stock isn't just about better entries — it's primarily about **scaling behavior**:

1. **Ross took 10+ round trips** in and out, locking profit at every level
2. **Bot took 5 trades** with all-or-nothing sizing
3. **Ross's position was dynamic** — heavy at the bottom, lighter at the top
4. **Bot's position was static** — same risk formula every time

If the bot could:
- Take partial profits at whole dollar levels and key resistance
- Add back on breakout confirmations
- Reduce size as price extends from the base
- Re-enter on VWAP reclaims without full structure reset

...it would capture significantly more of these moves.
