4. **More trading days = more opportunities.** 85 OOS days produced 81 trades vs 38 on 54 in-sample days. The strategy finds setups consistently across different market regimes.

5. **Drawdown is manageable.** Max DD of $5,131 (6.9%) on $30K starting equity, with quick recovery. Well within the 15% red flag threshold.

6. **Profit factor 4.17** — for every $1 lost, the bot makes $4.17. This holds across both in-sample and OOS.
