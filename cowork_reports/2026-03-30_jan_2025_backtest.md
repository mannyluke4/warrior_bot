## Key Patterns

### 1. The Strategy Works Out-of-Sample
+51% in one month on data the strategy never trained on. The squeeze detection → 2R target exit pattern holds on 2025 small-cap stocks.

### 2. sq_target_hit Remains Undefeated
8 out of 8 target hits were winners in Jan 2025, adding to the 39/39 record in YTD 2026. Combined: **47/47 on sq_target_hit across 80+ trading days.**

### 3. Win Rate Lower Than 2026 (68% vs 82%)
More para_trail losses in 2025 — the strategy takes more "probe" entries that get stopped out before finding the winner. But the winners are large enough (avg +$1,297) to overcome the losses (avg -$244) with a 5.3:1 win/loss ratio.

### 4. Cascade Pattern Works
GDTC (2 entries), PHIO (3 entries), VNCE (3 entries), ALUR (3 entries), AMOD (3 entries) — the bot correctly cascades into confirmed runners with the same pattern as 2026 (VERO, ROLR, EEIQ).

### 5. 7 Symbol-Dates Had No IBKR Tick Data
HOOK, PMAX, VS, VNCE, ALUR, ATXI, FMST returned "no ticks" from IBKR — likely delisted or ticker changed since Jan 2025. These stocks may have been tradeable at the time but can't be backtested now.
