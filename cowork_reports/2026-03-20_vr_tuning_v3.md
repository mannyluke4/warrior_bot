### Root Cause (Final Assessment)

The VWAP reclaim pattern — run above VWAP, dip below, recover above with volume — is **genuinely rare in micro-cap gap-up momentum stocks.** These stocks follow two dominant patterns:

1. **Runners** (VERO, ROLR): Gap up, run straight above VWAP all session. Never dip below. → Squeeze + MP territory.
2. **Faders** (GRI, APVO, TWG, TNMG, ELAB, ENVB): Gap up, initial run, then collapse below VWAP permanently. Never recover. → No opportunity for any strategy.

The "dip and recover" pattern that VR targets sits between these two — and it may simply not occur often enough in the micro-cap universe to justify a strategy module. Ross's VWAP reclaim trades may come from mid-cap stocks or larger-float names that have more institutional support for recoveries.

### Recommendation

1. **Shelve VR** — keep code in place (WB_VR_ENABLED=0) but stop active development
2. **Pivot to Strategy 5 (Curl/Extension)** — higher priority per MASTER_TODO
3. **Revisit VR** when/if we expand to mid-cap or larger-float universe
4. **Do NOT run full YTD with VR** — 0 trades means 0 additional data
